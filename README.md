### Bridging the Rural-Urban Divide through AI & Gamification

**GaonSe** is an AI-powered community commerce platform designed for the **AI for Bharat Hackathon (Problem Statement 03)**. It empowers rural women entrepreneurs (Aahar Bazaar) by providing a voice-first, zero-barrier entry into urban markets, wrapped in an engaging pixel-art village experience for buyers.

---

## üöÄ The Problem
* **The Digital Divide:** Rural entrepreneurs find traditional e-commerce dashboards (SKUs, logistics, analytics) too complex.
* **Lack of Market Access:** High-quality local snacks (Achar, Papad, Sweets) struggle to reach city consumers.
* **The Trust Gap:** Urban buyers want authenticity but lack a personal connection to the rural creators.

## ‚ú® Our Solution: Two-Sided Innovation

### 1. For the Entrepreneur: AI Sahayak (Voice-First)
* **Zero-UI Experience:** No typing or complex navigation. An AI agent talks to the entrepreneur in their local dialect.
* **Order Management:** AI handles order tracking, notification, and customer queries via voice-to-speech.
* **Visual Polish:** AI automatically cleans up raw product photos and places them in a professional "Pixel-Art Shop" frame to preserve authenticity while maintaining the app's aesthetic.

### 2. For the Buyer: The Digital Homestead
* **Gamified Marketplace:** Instead of a list, buyers explore a pixelated "interactive village."
* **Personal Growth:** Every purchase helps the buyer grow their own "Digital Homestead" (unlocking Indian-themed assets like Charpais, Tractors, and Wells).
* **Direct Impact:** A transparent link between the snack on your plate and the woman who made it.

## üë©‚Äçüíª How it Works (The Flow)
1. **Seller** takes a photo of her snack and tells the AI: *"I have 5 boxes of Ladoo ready."*
2. **AI** cleans the photo, lists it in the Village Marketplace, and updates the inventory.
3. **Buyer** "walks" through the village, orders the Ladoo, and unlocks a "Mango Tree" for their digital garden.
4. **AI Sahayak** calls the Seller to confirm: *"Didi, order confirmed! Pickup scheduled for tomorrow."*
